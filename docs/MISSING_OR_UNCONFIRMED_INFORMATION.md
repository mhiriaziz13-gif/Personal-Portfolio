# Missing or Unconfirmed Information

The following details were intentionally omitted from public pages and CVs because they were not supplied or confirmed:

- Phone number.
- Language proficiency levels or certifications for French and English.
- Public GitHub, personal domain or additional portfolio links.
- Work authorisation or visa status for any country.
- Salary expectation.
- Exact quantified project outcomes such as time saved, conversion change, revenue change, campaign spend or number of users.
- Employer contact or referee details.
- Exact production deployment URL for this website.
- Any information protected by client or employer confidentiality, including invoice amounts, booking records, internal commercial rules and customer data.

Before publishing, set the real domain in `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`. Do not add missing details without verification.
